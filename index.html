Join us for a 14 day grouptrip around Lombok, Indonesia

Welcome to this platform, where we invite you to join us to the island of Lombok in the heart of Indonesia with 12 other like-minded individuals. This organised, exclusive backpacking grouptrip takes place from the 22nd october till the 5th november, 2024.

We invite you to get lost with us in the jungle, hike and camp vulcano Rinjani, go on a boatrip around the secret islands, go spend full days in local villas and party all night, learn local recipes in an indonesian cooking class, get surfing lessons, go on a snorkeling daytrip and so much more!

If you're someone who is intrested to explore the local life for 14 days, connect with beautiful nature and spend time with 12 other travelers, then you're in the right place.

Why Lombok?

Lombok is a mesmerizing island, a gem of Indonesia, where enchanting landscapes, turquoise waters, and the friendliest locals await.

Why should you join one of these trips?

A Grouptrip with random people like this, offers an opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals who share your enthusiasm for adventure and seeing something new. Together, we'll uncover the hidden treasures of the island, wander through secluded coves, bask in stunning sunsets, chase waterfalls and ricefields, and immerse ourselves in the local culture and cuisine. Whether you wish to travel alone or with friends, everyone is welcome to join us for an anventure of a lifetime.

14 Days itinerary:

Day 1 (22.10.)

Arrive to Lombok Airport,get picked up and taken to the beautiful surftown Kuta, where we will spend our first three days.

Day 2 (23.10.)

After breakfast we are heading to Selang Belonag beach where we will enjoy a day full of surfing classes, lunch, beach and sunset.

Day 3 (24.10.)

Today we go for another surfing day, but in this time at Tanjun Ann beach where we going to take a surf boat to find the perfect waves.

Day 4 (25.10.)

After breakfast we head to village Nambung where will see authentic local life with beautiful beaches. We stay in a villa close to the beach where we do a little party in the night.

Day 5 (26.10.)

After breakfast we will experience a breathtaking ride with stunning views over Lombok landscapes and todayys destination will be Sekotong where we can chill on the beach and wonder around.

Day 6 (27.10.)

After breakfast on the beach we go island hopping to small islands with snorkeling, swimming, having lunch and chilling on the boat.

Day 7, 8 (28-29.10.)

After breakfast on the beach we are moving to Senggigi, which is a coastline town with tons of beaches where we will spend two days in villa. Nearby we can surf, enjoy the mountains in our free time, our pool and beautiful house.

Day 9 (30.10.)

Villa breakfast and transport to the heart of Lombok, to our favorite little jungle village Tetebatu. In the afternoon we will explore around and have buffet dinner in the best local restaurant.

Day 10 (31.10.)

Secret island trip.(We can’t say more because is secret hihi)

Day 11 (1.11.)

Come back from secret island and enjoy an indonesian cooking class in the evening.

Day 12 (2.11.)

We hike up to the second summit (easier hike) to vulcano Rinjani.

Day 13 (3.11.)

Come back from Rinjani trek and rest

Day 14 (4.11.)

Optional walking tour around Tetebatu, visiting waterfalls, the monkey forest and enjoying a little jamming night party.

Day14 (5.11.)

back to Lombok airport.

FAQ:

How about flights and visa?

Flights

We recommend to book your flights to Lombok International Airport from whichever location you’re coming from. If you need help with looking for the best deals, you can contact us anytime. We prefer to book flights on Skyscanner.com or Kiwi.com, where you can ususally find the best deals and prices.

Visa

You can buy the “Visa on arrival” once you land in Lombok Airport, which will cost you 500.000 rupias (30 euros)

Extra expences outside the package

Some meals: When we have a free day and we are not together everyone would have to pay for their meal, which would be around 50 euros in total for the 14 days.

Backpack? Suitcase?

We suggest you to come with a max. 40 liter backpack so you dont have to buy extra checked in lagguage for the flight, but if you prefer to travel with a bigger backpack or suitcase, it’s up tp you:)

Accomodations

Our accomodations for 14 days will be homestays, hostels, villas and shared rooms.
